Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots gathering of tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global event, drawing innovators, developers, and industry leaders from across the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and inclusion in the technology sector. We believe in the power of community and knowledge-sharing to drive meaningful change.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Reyes

A pioneer in AI ethics, Dr. Reyes has led global conversations on responsible AI development. She shared insights on building inclusive tech communities.

Mark Chen

As CTO of QuantumSoft, Mark has revolutionized quantum computing applications. At TechCon 2022, he unveiled breakthrough algorithms.

Lina Patel

A global advocate for women in tech, Lina has empowered thousands through her programs and mentorship. Her 2023 TechCon talk focused on tech equity.
